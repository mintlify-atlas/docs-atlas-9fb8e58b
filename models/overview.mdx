## Complete Model Comparison

All models are optimized for Apple Silicon and run entirely on-device.

| Model | Provider | Size | Parameters | Quantization | Speed Score | Smart Score | Recommended | Smart Mode |
|-------|----------|------|------------|--------------|-------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| **Qwen3 ASR 0.6B (f32)** | FluidAudio | ~2.5 GB | 0.6B | FP32 CoreML | 4/5 | 4/5 | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| **Apple Speech** | Apple | Built-in | On-device | System | 5/5 | 3/5 | ❌ No | ❌ No |
| **Parakeet TDT 0.6B (v3)** | NVIDIA | N/A | 0.6B | CoreML | 5/5 | 4/5 | ❌ No | ❌ No |
| **Whisper Large V3 Turbo** | WhisperKit | ~1.6 GB | 809M | CoreML | 2/5 | 5/5 | ❌ No | ❌ No |
| **Whisper Tiny** | WhisperKit | ~150 MB | 39M | CoreML | 4/5 | 2/5 | ❌ No | ❌ No |
| **Voxtral Mini 3B (bf16)** | Voxtral Core | ~9.4 GB | 3B | BF16 | 2/5 | 5/5 | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| **Voxtral Mini 3B (8-bit)** | Voxtral Core | ~4.6 GB | 3B | 8-bit | 3/5 | 4/5 | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |

<Note>
**Speed Score**: 1 (slowest) to 5 (fastest) - measures transcription speed relative to real-time audio.

**Smart Score**: 1 (basic) to 5 (advanced) - measures transcription quality, accuracy, and intelligence.
</Note>

## Models by Provider

### FluidAudio

**Qwen3 ASR 0.6B (f32)** is Petal's default model, offering an excellent balance of speed and quality.

- **Size**: ~2.5 GB
- **Languages**: 30+ including Chinese dialects
- **Speed**: 4/5 - Fast transcription with minimal latency
- **Quality**: 4/5 - High accuracy across multiple languages
- **Use case**: Best all-around model for most users

### Apple

**Apple Speech (Built-in)** leverages Apple's native Speech framework available on macOS 26+.

- **Size**: No download required
- **Availability**: Only on supported Macs running macOS 26 or later
- **Speed**: 5/5 - Fastest transcription speed
- **Quality**: 3/5 - Good accuracy, system-optimized
- **Use case**: Quick transcriptions when you don't want to download models

<Info>
Apple Speech only appears in the model list if your Mac supports it. The system automatically checks compatibility.
</Info>

### NVIDIA

**Parakeet TDT 0.6B (v3)** is powered by NVIDIA's research and delivered via FluidAudio.

- **Parameters**: 0.6B
- **Ranking**: Top-ranked on Open ASR Leaderboard
- **Speed**: 5/5 - 110x real-time speed
- **Quality**: 4/5 - Excellent accuracy
- **Use case**: When you need both speed and accuracy

### WhisperKit

**Whisper Large V3 Turbo** and **Whisper Tiny** bring OpenAI's Whisper models to Petal via the WhisperKit framework.

<Accordion title="Whisper Large V3 Turbo">
  - **Size**: ~1.6 GB
  - **Parameters**: 809M
  - **Languages**: 99 languages supported
  - **Speed**: 2/5 - Slower but more accurate
  - **Quality**: 5/5 - Near-large model accuracy
  - **Use case**: Maximum accuracy across many languages
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Whisper Tiny">
  - **Size**: ~150 MB (smallest model)
  - **Parameters**: 39M
  - **Languages**: Multi-language support
  - **Speed**: 4/5 - Fast and lightweight
  - **Quality**: 2/5 - Basic accuracy
  - **Use case**: Extremely fast transcription with minimal storage
</Accordion>

### Voxtral Core

**Voxtral Mini 3B** models are based on Mistral's speech models and offer unique **smart transcription** capabilities.

<Accordion title="Voxtral Mini 3B (bf16)">
  - **Size**: ~9.4 GB (largest model)
  - **Parameters**: 3B
  - **Quantization**: BF16 (highest quality)
  - **Speed**: 2/5 - Slower due to large size
  - **Quality**: 5/5 - Highest quality transcription
  - **Smart Features**: ✅ Transcription, Q&A, Summarization
  - **Use case**: When you need smart features and maximum quality
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Voxtral Mini 3B (8-bit)">
  - **Size**: ~4.6 GB (quantized)
  - **Parameters**: 3B
  - **Quantization**: 8-bit (reduced memory)
  - **Speed**: 3/5 - Faster than bf16 version
  - **Quality**: 4/5 - High quality with slight compromise
  - **Smart Features**: ✅ Transcription, Q&A, Summarization
  - **Use case**: Smart features with lower memory requirements
</Accordion>

<Warning>
Only Voxtral models support **Smart Mode** for Q&A and summarization. All other models support verbatim transcription only.
</Warning>

## Download Requirements

Most models require a one-time download before use:

- **Apple Speech**: No download required (built into macOS 26+)
- **All other models**: Require download through Petal

See [Downloading Models](/models/downloading-models) for detailed instructions on managing model downloads.
