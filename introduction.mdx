Petal is a native macOS menu bar app for fast, local-first audio transcription. Designed for Apple Silicon Macs, Petal keeps your workflow on-device with support for multiple AI transcription models.

## Why Petal?

### Privacy-First Design
Petal is designed for local transcription workflows and keeps the experience on-device where possible. Your audio never leaves your Mac.

### Multiple AI Models
Choose from 7 different transcription engines based on your needs:
- **Apple Speech** - Built-in on supported Macs, no download required
- **Qwen3 ASR** - Fast multilingual transcription supporting 30+ languages (recommended)
- **Parakeet TDT** - Top-ranked accuracy with 110x real-time speed
- **Whisper Large V3 Turbo** - Near-large accuracy across 99 languages
- **Whisper Tiny** - Smallest and fastest Whisper model
- **Voxtral Mini 3B** - Advanced transcription with Q&A and summarization from voice

### Fast Workflow
Petal integrates seamlessly into your workflow:
- **Push-to-talk shortcut** (default: Option + Space) for instant recording
- **Menu bar access** for quick settings and history
- **Auto-paste** to copy transcriptions directly to your clipboard
- **Smart transcription** for refined output with custom prompts (Voxtral models)

### Native macOS Experience
Built specifically for macOS with:
- Menu bar interface that stays out of your way
- Optimized for Apple Silicon performance
- Automatic updates via Sparkle
- Clean, minimal design

## System Requirements

- macOS 14.0 or later
- Apple Silicon Mac (M1, M2, M3, M4, or newer)
- Microphone access permission
- Accessibility permission (for global keyboard shortcuts)

<Note>
Apple Speech transcription requires macOS 26 or later and is only available on supported devices.
</Note>
