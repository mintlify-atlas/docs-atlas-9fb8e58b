## Understanding Score Ratings

### Speed Score (1-5)

Measures how fast the model transcribes audio relative to real-time:

- **5/5**: Lightning fast (110x+ real-time) - Parakeet, Apple Speech
- **4/5**: Very fast - Qwen3, Whisper Tiny
- **3/5**: Moderate speed - Voxtral 8-bit
- **2/5**: Slower but accurate - Whisper Large V3, Voxtral bf16
- **1/5**: Slowest (none currently)

<Note>
Even "slower" models still transcribe faster than real-time on Apple Silicon Macs.
</Note>

### Smart Score (1-5)

Measures transcription quality, accuracy, and intelligence:

- **5/5**: Highest accuracy and quality - Whisper Large V3, Voxtral bf16
- **4/5**: Excellent accuracy - Qwen3, Parakeet, Voxtral 8-bit
- **3/5**: Good accuracy - Apple Speech
- **2/5**: Basic accuracy - Whisper Tiny
- **1/5**: Minimal accuracy (none currently)

## Switching Models

You can easily switch between models in Petal:

1. Models are selected in the app settings
2. Downloaded models remain available for quick switching
3. You can have multiple models downloaded simultaneously
4. Delete unused models to free up space

<Warning>
Switching to an undownloaded model will require downloading it first. This can take several minutes depending on model size and internet speed.
</Warning>
